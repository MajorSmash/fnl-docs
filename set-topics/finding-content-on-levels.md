🖐️ 
Sorry about the mess: ninja has been developed through years, new versions usually adding content / reworking old ones / merging separate usecases.
However: there is a bit of order in this chaos 🤓 
.
1. Levels are stored under *Tutorial/Levels* & *Usecases/Levels* --- tuts: less pimp, schematic, more educative value / usecases: somewhat polished
This playlist shows USECASES: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLVCUepYV6TvO6QhpqaT1GRVGC96QMcUJs
This playlist shows TUTS: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLVCUepYV6TvPYbofpEf_ghznfihM-yt-B

2. Level names are quite telling! eg.: "FIRE", "VOLUMEFOG", "VOLCANO", "LAVA", "COASTLINE"... etc
3. Level content is always placed on NUMBERED STAGES - and there is usually an explanative text of what the given stage does. (eg.: "Campfire", "Candle")
4. Live 1.7 was a major update. Re-worked and new levels: have *"LIVE17"* as the last part in their names. Once opened: yellow textblock at startzone explains what's new
5. For the older levels: here is this walkthrough: sometimes I use this to point users to certain stages: https://youtu.be/0O11SNavhM4
6. In case you are often looking up ninja contents: you could make your own notes (eg.: Level 2A, Stage 13: puncture!)
