**SET TOPIC: LARGE WATER BODIES SIMULATION**
 welcome! 
Ninja is made to "add on top" of world-generators: it handles interactions via fluid-simulation, and the result could be added on top of "non-interactive fields".
In this video, ninja has been added "on top of" UE native waves: https://youtu.be/-OvCw4Y4480
Here, ninja runs in standalone mode: no world generator, just a plain surface with ninja running: https://youtu.be/ry5ckYEM0uc
This video explains how it is done: https://youtu.be/f2Sqjzf4KKg
.
Of course, ninja could be used as a world-generator, as well: this CLOUD field example demonstrates how to generate tiled patterns to populate infinite fields: https://youtu.be/uH2DRdKruaU
The same (as, with clouds) could be done with water - but, we have not made a use-case for this, yet (coming in 4 weeks: "ninja infinite water field gen example").
