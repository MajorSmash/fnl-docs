Hi! 🖐️ SET TOPIC: **DECAL vs WATER**
1. ninja water bodies (eg. river) use Unreal's standard "SingleLayerWater" shader
2. UE decals, by default, do NOT work on SingleLayerWater
3. Here is a workaround: https://twitter.com/dmeville/status/1512909533567352835
