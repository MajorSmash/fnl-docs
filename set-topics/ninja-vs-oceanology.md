SET TOPIC: **NINJA vs OCEANOLOGY**
Ninja is originally not a "world generator" - but a local fluidsim for character and area FX. Recently, we have started to experiment how to add local FX on top of *world-scale* patterns. Our first test with UE native water was successful: https://youtu.be/-OvCw4Y4480.
Yet, we** have not tested ninja with any other systems (Like Cartoon Water Shader or Oceanology)** -- in theory, it could be added much the same way, as with UE water. Next move: we would like to demonstrate how to use ninja as a world-scale pattern generator (coming with UseCase 4/5).
