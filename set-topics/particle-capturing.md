🖐️ 
Let us look at the setup: we have two emitters. 
1. One (on the right) is like regular emitter, throwing particles (and writing particle velocity to particle color, RGB being XYZ velocity - using the "Set2DVelocityColor" module)
2. The other emitter (on the left) is reading the attributes (position and color) of the particles (using the "AttributeReader" module), generated by the right emitter - and writes the position and velocity information to a RenderTarget (using the WriteGridTo RenderTarget module)
