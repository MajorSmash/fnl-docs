SET TOPIC: **NINJATOOLS FLOWMAPS** <:NinjaTools:851394765647118346> 
 your question is relevant! (1) Velocity and Density maps are generated in the same process / derived from the same simulation. Normally, we use them together / it is not advised to use "velo from sim 1 and density from sim 2". ----- (2) still, velocity maps could be used MANY more ways than density maps / independently from their "density pair" ---> the whole FLOWMAP concept is about "pushing things" (be it pixels or particles) using a velocity field --- and the NinjaFlow module / FlowPlayer is utilising this concept (eg. you could make a static texture move using a flowmap --- say: you have a NOISE tile pattern - and you drive it with a velo map, so, we have a nice noise advection --- and you add the original, lowres density on top: in this case the flowmap is adding "details to the flow")
