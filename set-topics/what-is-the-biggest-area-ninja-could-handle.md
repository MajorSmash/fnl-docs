1. ninja runs a local, finite sized sim (...of user defined size: 1m - 1000m)
2. attaches the sim to the player - so, it is always "surrounding the player" - whereever it goes
3. handles the surface OUTSIDE the sim area using non-interactive pattern generators.
Explained here: https://youtu.be/S5n5Tpd1Gko?list=PLVCUepYV6TvMXN8HQjLU9wKz-G_6JvJsF&t=598
.
