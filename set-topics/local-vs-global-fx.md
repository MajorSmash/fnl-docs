SET TOPIC: **LOCAL vs GLOBAL FX**, using ninja
- Ninja is initially made to deliver **LOCAL **vfx ---  for (1) characters (eg. spells, flames) and (2) area fx eg. portals, pools)
- it is just now (release 1.5), that we have moved out from the comfort zone, and take our first step towards **GLOBAL **vfx
.
- for global usage, the recommended way is to add ninja on top of some well managed global system (eg. UE water)
- of course ninja is capable of delivering global fields as well (like full sky cloud cover: https://youtu.be/uH2DRdKruaU) - and a full sea could be made similar ways, but doing this with ninja would be like building worlds from lego-1-bricks ---> so many good packages (eg. ultra dynamic sky, oceanography) to handle clouds and water on a global scale - I would keep ninja in this dedicated role of delivering vfx when something well defined / choregraphed happens --- eg.: kraken emerges from the sea, a large cloud-vortex forms around a titan...etc.
