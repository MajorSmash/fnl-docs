SET TOPIC: **NINJA WATER SIM**
Dear Oceanology / Ninja users!
Here comes a brief summary on what ninja does with water.

(1) Ninja is a simple, 2D, generic fluidsim that could be driven by objects, particles, textures, materials, webcam... etc.
(2) Ninja simulation buffers (density, velocity, pressure...etc) are EXPOSED to users
(3) how the final visuals look like: depends on how you are using the sim buffers. It could be smoke, fire or liquid like.
(4) lately, we have created SIX Use Case packages, that demonstrate how sim buffers could be used to create "water like" phenomena.
.
The experiments are all explained / showcased on this playlist with NINE videos: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLVCUepYV6TvO6QhpqaT1GRVGC96QMcUJs
It is best to start from the bottom (Video No9, "FluidNinja LIVE 1.5 - Moving in WorldSpace") and move "upwards" - to understand the dev process
.
